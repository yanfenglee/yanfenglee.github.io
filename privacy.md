# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Streak13 ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

## Information We Collect

Streak13 does **not collect personal information** from users.

All habit tracking data created in the app is stored **locally on your device** and is not transmitted to our servers.

## How We Use Information

Since the app does not collect personal information, we do not use, process, or store user data on external servers.

The information stored locally on your device is only used to provide the core functionality of the app, such as:

* Habit tracking
* Streak counting
* Daily progress monitoring

## Third-Party Services

Streak13 may use standard Apple system services required for app distribution and analytics provided by Apple.

These services may collect anonymous usage data according to Apple's privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

We do **not sell, trade, or share personal information** with third parties.

## Data Security

All data created within Streak13 remains on your device. We do not maintain a remote database of user information.

## Children's Privacy

Streak13 does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that we can address the issue.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
